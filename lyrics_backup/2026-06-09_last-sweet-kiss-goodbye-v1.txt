[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I trace the shadows falling cross your face
This midnight hour is our secret place
Just pull me closer, let the world just fade
And let me taste the promises we made

[Verse 1]
We parked out where the streetlights could not pry
Beneath a blanket of a starry sky
Your hands are resting gently on my chest
Of all the moments, I love this the best

[Pre-Chorus]
I feel the fire catching in your breath
We are dancing on the edge of life and death
I want to memorize the way you sigh
Before the morning comes to say goodbye

[Chorus]
So give me one last sweet kiss goodbye
Beneath the canvas of the fading sky
Just leave your memory painted on my soul
And take a piece of me to make you whole

[Post-Chorus]
